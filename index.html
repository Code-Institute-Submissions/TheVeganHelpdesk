A beginners guide to going plant-based | For noobs, dabblers and hardcores alike

We are a merry band of chill vegans - both full and part time - looking to spread the good word ONLY to those who want to hear it! As with anything, this is a lifestyle that should be a choice, and we would like to make that as accessible to as many people as we possibly can. We believe that how you go about attempting plant based foods is your own choice. There's a lot of info out there that can make the transition seem daunting and it absolutely doesn't have to be! So! Let's begin!

Have you been toying with the idea of going plant based since seeing all the delicious instagram posts last Veganuary? Are you aware of how your food habits impact the environment and want to make changes to reduce this impact? Do you feel overly reliant on meat and want to include other options into your diet? Are you unsure whether going full vegan is for you but don't know the best way to find out? Did you adopt a pet pig and are now battling feelings of guilt and shame? :P

Fortunately, it has never been easier to be vegan in Ireland. With the popularity of Veganuary, supermarkets are taking notice of the "trend" and are working to provide more and more plant-based options. While sure, these count more as "fast food" sometimes, it's real good to have the options of treats! They are also a great way to make the transition as your teeth start to miss meat. Trust me, your teeth will miss it the most, but not for long! It's not all just meat replacements though. Theres a whole world of natural foods out there that contain the proteins and vitamins you need to make the transition safely and deliciously!

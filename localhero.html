Oa'tisDelicious

Founded late last year, Oa'tisDelicious is a plant based company that started when long-time-vegan owner, Sally, got tired of learning how much most alternative 'milks' were costing the environment. So she started making her own oat milk and supplying it locally. Oat milk uses the least water to manufacture from start to finish and has a deliciously creamy, foamable consistancy. Sally uses guaranteed gluten free oats and brews the milk to order. Oa'tisDelicious has now expanded to include a vegan/gluten free bakery with local seasonal treats available every day.

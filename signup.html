About us

We are a merry band of chill vegans - both full and part time - looking to spread the good word ONLY to those who want to hear it! As with anything, this is a lifestyle that should be a choice, and we would like to make that as accessible to as many people as we possibly can. We believe that how you go about attempting plant based foods is your own choice. Welcome to the green side!
